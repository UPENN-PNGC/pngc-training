# Contributing to PNGC Training

Thank you for your interest in registering a PNGC Training event!

## How to Register a Course

1. Check that your event meets the [requirements](#requirements-for-registration)
2. Click on **Issues** â†’ **New Issue**
3. Select **"Register Course"**
4. Fill out the template
5. Submit the issue
6. A maintainer will review and add your course to the repository

## Requirements for Registration

### Required Information

Course must be part of an official PNGC Training series.

- **Course Title**: Full title of the course
- **Year**: Year the course is offered (e.g., 2026)
- **Semester**: Spring, Summer, or Fall
- **Contact Email**: For course communication
- **Binder Requirement**: Indicate if a Binder environment is needed

### Recommended

- **Additional Information**: Any relevant links or details

## Questions?

Contact the PNGC Training maintainers through GitHub issues or [contact information].
